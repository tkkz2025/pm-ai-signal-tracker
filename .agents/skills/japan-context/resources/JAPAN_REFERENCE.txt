## Japan adoption dynamics

### Why adoption is slow
- Ringi (稟議) consensus decision-making — all stakeholders must approve
- Lifetime employment norms — AI threatens jobs, creates resistance
- Risk aversion culture — prefer proven solutions over pioneering
- Language barrier — English-first AI tools miss the Japanese enterprise
- Vendor lock-in concerns — prefer in-house or partner solutions
- Data privacy strictness — even within organisations

### Why adoption is accelerating
- Severe labour shortage — AI is a workforce necessity, not a nice-to-have
- Government mandate — explicit government push removes political risk
- Proven global case studies — Japan enterprises follow once others have proven
- Major infrastructure investment — AWS, Azure, Google removing data residency objections
- Local model availability — NTT, Fujitsu, SoftBank removing language barriers

### The 12-18 month lag rule
Global AI use case deployment → Japan enterprise awareness: ~6 months
Japan enterprise awareness → pilot: ~6-12 months
Japan enterprise pilot → production: ~6-12 months
Total: 18-36 months from global proof to Japan production

Exception: Government-mandated use cases (faster)
Exception: Labour-shortage-driven use cases (faster)
Exception: Use cases where Japanese vendor leads globally (skip lag)
